About Me

I am Kiran Thengil, currently a postdoctoral researcher in condensed matter theory at SISSA, Trieste, Italy, working with Prof. Giuseppe E. Santoro.

My research interests include condensed matter theory, quantum many-body physics, QAOA, quantum annealing, quantum thermodynamics, quantum heat engines, shortcuts to adiabaticity, counter-diabatic driving, and numerical simulations.

  • M.Sc. in Physics, Loyola College, India (2015–2017)
  • B.Sc. in Physics, Christ College, India
